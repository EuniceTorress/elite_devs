Resource Generation Office

The Resource Generation Office (RGO) of Batangas State University, is committed to serving the needs of students, faculty & staff, and the entire BatStateU community. By enhancing University services and providing quality products, RGO plays a vital role in generating additional income to strengthen the institution's financial capacity. It offers a wide range of services, including affordable school uniforms, books sourced directly from authors or publishers to ensure the best value. RGO also offers innovative facilities at reasonable fees, and operates a University shop featuring quality souvenirs and merchandise.
